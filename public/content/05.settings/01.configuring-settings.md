# Settings

Configure your Delivery Hub experience through the Settings tab.

## Accessing Settings

1. Open the **Delivery Hub** app
2. Click the **Settings** tab
3. You'll see cards for different configuration areas

## Available Settings

### General Settings

Control how Delivery Hub behaves for your organization.

### Jira Settings

Configure the connection to Jira Cloud (if your organization uses Jira).

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Jira Enabled** | Turn Jira sync on or off |
| **Instance URL** | Your Jira Cloud address |
| **Project Key** | Default project for new issues |

To test your connection:
1. Enter your Jira details
2. Click **Test Connection**
3. A success message confirms it's working

### AI Settings

Configure the AI-powered ticket enhancement feature.

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Suggestions Enabled** | Turn AI features on or off |
| **AI Estimation Enabled** | Include effort estimates in AI suggestions |

To test the AI connection:
1. Enter your API key
2. Click **Test Connection**
3. A success message confirms it's working

## Saving Changes

After making changes:
1. Click **Save** on the settings card
2. Wait for the confirmation message
3. Your changes take effect immediately

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Test Failed

If a connection test fails:
1. Verify your credentials are correct
2. Check that required services are accessible
3. Contact your administrator if issues persist

### Settings Not Taking Effect

Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and back in
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Contact your administrator

### Missing Settings

Some settings may only be visible to administrators. If you need access to a setting you can't see, contact your Delivery Hub administrator.

## Getting Help

If you need assistance with settings:
1. Note what you're trying to configure
2. Capture any error messages
3. Contact your Delivery Hub administrator
